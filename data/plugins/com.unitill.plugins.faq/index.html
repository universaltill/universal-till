Frequently Asked Questions

This page is provided by the external plugin com.unitill.plugins.faq.

How do I add products?
Open Designer and add label, code, price, and optionally an image.
How do I change currency and tax?
Go to Settings and update currency, tax rate, and inclusive/exclusive mode.
Where are plugins managed?
Install from the Plugins page. This FAQ is installed into the device under data/plugins/com.unitill.plugins.faq and served at /plug/com.unitill.plugins.faq.
