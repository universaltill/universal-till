FAQ

General

How do I add products?
Go to Designer, add label/code/price (and optional image), then use tiles on the home page.
How do I change currency and tax?
Open Settings and update currency, tax rate, and inclusive/exclusive mode.

Plugins

What are plugins?
Plugins extend the till with integrations, hardware, and pages like this FAQ. They are listed in the Store and installed to the device.
